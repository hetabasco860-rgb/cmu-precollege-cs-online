Program Overview

The Pre-College Computer Science Online program is an intensive online experience designed for highly motivated high school students seeking a rigorous introduction to university-level computer science.

Instruction is delivered across 20 structured sessions, spanning AP Computer Science A, AP Computer Science Principles, and advanced computer science topics beyond the AP curriculum.

Learning Focus

Students develop computational thinking, programming proficiency, and foundational theory through guided instruction, applied practice, and project-based milestones.

Detailed course objectives and curriculum structure are available on the Course page.

Certificate of Completion

Students who successfully complete the program and submit the final project will receive a Certificate of Completion issued by Pre-College Programs, Department of Computer Science.

The final project is evaluated from a single official submission. Requirements and evaluation criteria are outlined on the Projects page.
