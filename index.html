Pre-College Computer Science Online

Carnegie Mellon University

Program Overview

The Pre-College Computer Science Online program is an intensive, university-level online experience designed for motivated high school students with a strong interest in computer science.

The program consists of 20 structured instructional sessions covering AP Computer Science A, AP Computer Science Principles, and advanced computer science topics beyond the AP curriculum.

Certificate

Students who successfully complete the program and submit a final project will receive an official Certificate of Completion issued by the Pre-College Programs, Department of Computer Science.
