Apply Intro

The Pre-College Computer Science Online program is designed for students prepared to engage in rigorous, structured computer science instruction at the pre-university level.

Instruction is delivered across 20 structured sessions, covering AP Computer Science A, AP Computer Science Principles, and advanced topics beyond the AP curriculum.

Completion of the program requires submission of a final project. The final project is evaluated through a single official submission.

Tuition & Payment

Tuition for the Pre-College Computer Science Online program is fixed at USD $500. All payment methods listed below are supported for authorization.
