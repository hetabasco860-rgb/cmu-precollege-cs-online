Application Workflow

The application workflow is designed to guide students through program information, eligibility considerations, and enrollment steps.

Students should review program expectations carefully prior to proceeding to payment.

Step 1: Introduction

The program is delivered online across 20 sessions and includes structured instruction, practice, and a culminating Final Project. Students seeking a Certificate of Completion must submit the Final Project according to the requirements on the Projects page.

Continue to Step 2 →

Step 2: Apply Now

Applicants are expected to have the maturity and time commitment required for an intensive online computer science program. Coursework includes programming assignments and project-based milestones that build toward a final submission.

Applicants are encouraged to review the Course and Projects pages prior to enrollment: Course · Projects

Step 3: Complete Payment

Tuition for the program is USD $500. Accepted payment methods are listed on the Payment page.
