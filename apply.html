Apply Intro

The Pre-College Computer Science Online program is designed for students prepared to engage in rigorous, structured learning. The program spans 20 instructional sessions and includes project-based milestones culminating in a Final Project submission.

Final Project policy: The Final Project is evaluated from a single official submission. Students should plan, test, and document carefully prior to submission.

Applicants are strongly encouraged to review: Course and Final Project requirements.

Program Expectations

Students are expected to:

  • Engage consistently with instruction and assigned practice between sessions.
  • Maintain clear code organization and documentation standards.
  • Demonstrate academic integrity and independent understanding of submitted work.
  • Complete a final software artifact that meets scope, correctness, and communication requirements.

Tuition & Payment Details

Tuition for the program is USD $500. Full payment is required to complete enrollment.
